起 · 源

问天，取自《楚辞·天问》，即叩问苍穹，探索浩瀚宇宙，探求未知奥秘。寓意网安人对真理追求的坚韧与执着，探求网安真理征途漫漫，追求网安创新永无止境。 问天安全团队创立于2022年，目前团队7人，成员分布于奇安信、360、等国内网安企业，团队注重web漏洞挖掘，渗透测试和CTF竞赛。

Wen tian(QT) is taken from "Song of Ch - Tian Wen", that is, to ask the sky, to explore the vast universe, to explore the mysteries of the unknown. It symbolizes the tenacity and persistence of cyber security practitioners in the pursuit of truth, the long journey to seek the truth of cyber security, and the never-ending pursuit of cyber security innovation.

The team was founded in 2022 and currently consists of 7 members from Qi'anxin, 360, and other domestic cyber security companies. The team focuses on web vulnerability mining, penetration testing and CTF competitions.
